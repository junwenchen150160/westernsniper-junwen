About Western Sniper

Western Sniper is an exciting shooting game that takes you back to the Wild West era. As a skilled sharpshooter, your mission is to eliminate targets with precision and speed.

Set in authentic Western environments, you'll face increasingly challenging scenarios that test your aim and reflexes. The stunning visuals and immersive sound effects create an authentic frontier atmosphere.

Whether you're a casual gamer or an experienced shooter fan, Western Sniper offers engaging gameplay that's easy to learn but challenging to master.
