About Western Sniper

Western Sniper is an exciting shooting game that takes you back to the era of the Wild West. As a skilled sharpshooter, your mission is to eliminate targets with precision and speed.

Set in authentic western environments, you'll face increasingly challenging scenarios that test your aiming abilities and reaction time.
